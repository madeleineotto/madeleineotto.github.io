Save the Penguins exists to make a difference in the lives of young, downy penguin chicks everywhere. Climate change is impacting the Antarctic Peninsula at a rapid clip. When it gets warm, it rains. When it rains, penguin chicks die. You can help by insulating your house, carpool, reuse items, etc.
